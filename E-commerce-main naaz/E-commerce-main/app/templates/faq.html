Frequently Asked Questions

1) Can I change my shipping address after placing the order?

No, the shipping address cannot be changed once the order is placed. Please contact customer support for assistance.

2) How do I register on FusionFits?

You can sign up by providing your username, email, password, role, contact, address, city, and date of birth.

3) How do I place an order?

Select the product, add it to the cart, proceed to checkout, enter delivery details, and complete payment.

4) How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

5) How can I contact customer support?

You can reach out via email, chat support, or call our helpline available on the Contact Us page.

6) What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, wallets, and cash on delivery (COD) where applicable.

7) Can I change my email or contact details after registering?

Yes, you can update your profile details under the "User Account" section.

8) How do I search for a product?

Use the search bar on the home page. Related products will also be displayed.

9) Can I delete my account permanently?

Yes, you can delete your account under "Account Settings." Note that this action is irreversible.

10) How do I update my profile details?

Navigate to "My Account" > "Edit Profile" to update your name, address, contact details, and preferences.

11) Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary based on location.

13) What is your return policy?

FAQs for Delivery Personnel

1) How do I receive new delivery assignments?

New delivery assignments will be sent via the FusionFits delivery app. You will receive notifications for new orders.

2) What should I do if I cannot deliver an order?

If you are unable to deliver an order, inform the dispatch center immediately and update the order status in the app.

3) How do I handle a customer dispute?

Contact customer support and provide the necessary details. Never argue with the customer and remain professional.

4) What happens if the customer is unavailable?

Try contacting the customer via the provided phone number. If they remain unreachable, follow company guidelines for undelivered orders.

5) How do I update order status?

Use the FusionFits delivery app to mark orders as picked up, in transit, or delivered.

6) What should I do if I have a vehicle breakdown?

Inform the dispatch team immediately and request assistance. Arrange for an alternate vehicle if possible.

7) How do I ensure customer safety during delivery?

Always verify the delivery details and follow company protocols for contactless or secure deliveries.

8) Can I refuse a delivery assignment?

Refusal is allowed only in specific situations, such as vehicle issues or emergencies. Inform the dispatch team if you are unable to deliver.

9) What should I do in case of an accident while delivering?

Ensure your safety first, contact emergency services if needed, and report the incident to the dispatch team.

10) How do I handle cash-on-delivery (COD) payments?

Always count the cash in front of the customer and provide a receipt. Follow company guidelines for handling COD payments.

11) How do I navigate to the delivery location?

Use the in-app navigation feature or Google Maps for accurate directions.

12) What should I do if a customer refuses to accept the order?

Politely ask for a reason, update the app with the status, and return the item if necessary.

13) How do I handle damaged products?

Report any damages before delivery and contact support for guidance.

14) How do I report late deliveries?

Use the app to update the estimated delivery time and inform the dispatch team.

15) What is the dress code for deliveries?

Wear company-provided uniforms or adhere to the professional dress code policy.
